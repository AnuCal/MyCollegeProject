Lotus garden is amongst the most well-known catering businesses in Sacramento, CA. Since opening back in 2015 by a skilled team, we have evolved continually. Chef Anika (a two-time Award nominee for Best Chef) created a menu inspired by her childhood in India and her culinary training she got from Institute of Hotel Management and catering Institute.

At Lotus garden, we are constantly pushing ourselves to find new ways to redefine the culinary experience, and we have embraced a curated selection of dishes meant to be shared. Do try us out!
